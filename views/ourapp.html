Experience and Innovation leads to success

This application has its antecedent in the Gadget launched in 2006: Forget + iPod Sports Kid. The tool helped runners to measure distance and speed. With these innovative marketing strategies, Forget becomes more than just a sporting goods brand Forget's marketing strategy positions it as an entity that motivates, trains and empowers to the masses. However, innovation as the main marketing strategy lies in the product itself. Forget launched the first innovation in its products in 1979, introducing pad technology in footwear. With this technology, footwear sports shoe has air-filled plastic membranes that provide more comfort to the athlete during training. Later, in 1987, Forget released Air pro; these contained two bags of compressed air that absorbed the impacts during jumps or walks. These bags can be viewed through a special window at the bottom of the shoe. forget later customized the product in such a way that users could easily select their footwear depending on the sport they practiced.
